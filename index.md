Privacy Policy for Left

Effective Date: March 2026

Left is designed to be private by default.

DATA COLLECTION

Left does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data.

All information entered into the app — including bills, income details, and savings preferences — is stored locally on your device using Apple’s native storage technologies.

NETWORK ACCESS

Left does not require an internet connection.
The app does not communicate with any external servers.

ACCOUNTS

Left does not require user accounts.
No sign-in is required.

THIRD-PARTY SERVICES

Left does not use analytics, advertising, or third-party tracking services.

DATA DELETION

If you delete the app, all associated data is permanently removed from your device.
